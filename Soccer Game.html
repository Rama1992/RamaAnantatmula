Soccer Game

Men's Soccer game was conducted at "Caniglia Field" between UNO and UCLA teams on Saturday event at 7:30 P.M. As students we had a free entry to the game with the MAVCARD. This was my first ever Soccer game. Prior to the game, we had a "Soccer Pregame Party" at Pep Bowl where we had fun with games, music, free food and lots of MavSWAG!
